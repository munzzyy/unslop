The integrated hydration sensor learns your baseline (weight, activity level, climate) and quietly monitors your water intake through passive tracking—no logging required. The bottle itself is borosilicate glass wrapped in food-grade silicone. It's durable, dishwasher safe, and doesn't taste like plastic. The cap is a smooth open-close mechanism with zero friction points to get stuck or degrade. We tested it against 50,000 cycles.
